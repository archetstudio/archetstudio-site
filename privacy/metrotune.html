1. Introduction

Archet Studio LLC ("Archet Studio," "we," "our," or "us") develops and operates MetroTune™, a mobile application that provides metronome and chromatic tuning capabilities for musicians.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use the MetroTune™ application or visit our website at https://archetstudio.com, and describes your privacy rights and choices.

2. Scope

This policy applies to the MetroTune™ mobile application developed and published by Archet Studio LLC, including versions distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, as well as the Archet Studio LLC website (archetstudio.com) as it relates to MetroTune™. This policy applies globally to all users regardless of location, with jurisdiction-specific rights described in Section 10.

3. Current Data Collection

This policy describes the data practices of MetroTune™ during normal operation and distribution through public app stores. The only data we directly collect is through direct email contact. This includes messages sent to any of our email addresses: inquiry@archetstudio.com (general inquiries), support@archetstudio.com (app support), and privacy@archetstudio.com (privacy requests). If you contact us for support, we receive the information you voluntarily provide, such as your email address and any technical details (device type, OS version) you include. We use this information only to resolve your technical issues and improve our products. We do not sell this information or use it for marketing purposes. Email correspondence is retained for 12 months after the inquiry is resolved, after which it is permanently deleted.

Data we do not collect:

  • Your name or any in-app account credentials
  • Email addresses entered within the app
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS data
  • Photos, camera, or video content
  • Precise or coarse geolocation
  • Payment information or financial data
  • Biometric identifiers or health data
  • Browsing history or cross-app behavior

4. Application Data Practices

MetroTune™ adheres to the Privacy Manifest and Data Minimization principles outlined in this document. The following describes the data practices built into the application:

4.1 Microphone Audio (Transient)

MetroTune™ accesses the device microphone for real-time audio analysis, including pitch detection (tuner) and optional hands-free control via double-clap detection (metronome). All audio is processed entirely in-memory on the user's device using pure signal processing. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. No speech recognition is performed. We do not derive or infer your identity, biometrics, or any personal characteristics from audio data.

4.2 User Preferences (Local Only)

MetroTune™ stores user preferences (such as color theme and premium unlock status) locally on the device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves the device and is not transmitted to any server.

4.3 Third-Party SDK Data

MetroTune™ integrates the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to serve advertisements. Ad personalization is determined dynamically at startup based on user consent and platform tracking permissions (see Section 7 for the full logic). The AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers — AAID (Android Advertising ID), available by default unless the user opts out via device settings. IDFA (iOS Identifier for Advertisers), requested at startup via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework; used for ad personalization only if the user grants permission.
  • IP address
  • App interaction data (ad views, taps, rewarded ad completions)
  • SDK diagnostic telemetry — standard operational diagnostics collected by the AdMob SDK; no separate crash reporting SDK is integrated

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy. We do not have direct access to the personal data Google collects through its SDK.

4.4 Rewarded Ad Value Exchange

MetroTune™ offers a 24-hour premium content unlock in exchange for watching a rewarded video advertisement. When a user completes a rewarded ad, we process the ad completion event to grant temporary access to premium features (e.g., additional sound packs).

  • The ad completion timestamp is stored locally on the device via AsyncStorage
  • Access expires automatically after 24 hours
  • No payment information, account credentials, or personal data is collected during this exchange
  • The AdMob SDK records the rewarded ad conversion event for ad network reporting

4.5 Geolocation Data

MetroTune™ does not collect Precise Geolocation Data. In the event that any future feature requires temporary location access, it will be accompanied by a clear, obvious on-screen indicator as required by applicable law.

5. Data Storage & Security

Archet Studio LLC does not operate its own backend servers or cloud storage. User preferences and app state are stored locally on the device. Ad data is transmitted to Google's servers by the AdMob SDK.

  • Local storage: Preferences are stored via AsyncStorage (SQLite on Android, NSUserDefaults on iOS). This data is non-sensitive — containing only UI preferences such as color theme and a premium unlock timestamp, with no personal identifiers — and is therefore not encrypted at rest.
  • Network security: All network connections (AdMob) are enforced over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Data retention: Local data persists until the user uninstalls the application, at which point all local data is permanently deleted. Data collected by the AdMob SDK is retained according to Google's data retention policies. We do not control Google's retention schedule. To review and manage data Google holds about you, visit Google's Data & Privacy controls.
  • Data breach notification: In the event of a data breach affecting personal information, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law, including within 72 hours for GDPR-covered incidents and as required under California Civil Code § 1798.82.

7. Advertising & Tracking

MetroTune™ displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Ad personalization is determined dynamically at startup based on the following logic:

Region Platform Ads served
Non-EUAndroidPersonalized (AAID)
Non-EUiOS — ATT grantedPersonalized (IDFA)
Non-EUiOS — ATT deniedNon-personalized
EU/EEA/UKAny — consented + ATT grantedPersonalized
EU/EEA/UKAny — declined or ATT deniedNon-personalized
Error / fallbackAnyNon-personalized (safe default)

The AAID (Android) and IDFA (iOS, if granted) may be used by the AdMob SDK to serve personalized advertisements and to track ad conversions that unlock premium content (see Section 4.4).

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

MetroTune™ implements the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework on iOS. At first launch, iOS users are presented with an ATT permission prompt with the usage description: "MetroTune uses this permission to show you more relevant ads. Denying will result in less relevant ads." If permission is granted, the IDFA may be used by the AdMob SDK for personalized ad serving and conversion measurement. If permission is denied, all ads are served without the IDFA (non-personalized).

MetroTune™ does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

EU/EEA/UK Consent (Google UMP)

For users located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, MetroTune™ implements the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent framework. At first launch (or when consent status has lapsed), a Google-hosted consent form is presented. This form explains the categories of data collected, the purposes of processing, and the third-party partners (including Google/AdMob) that may access data. Personalized ads are served to EU/EEA/UK users only if explicit consent is given and ATT permission is granted (iOS). If consent is declined, ads are served without advertising identifiers (non-personalized).

Ad Supply Chain Transparency

Our ad supply chain is authorized via an app-ads.txt file hosted at archetstudio.com/app-ads.txt, identifying Archet Studio LLC as a DIRECT publisher (ID: pub-8090048526672531). Google's TAG (Trustworthy Accountability Group) certification authority ID f08c47fec0942fa0 is included to provide users and advertisers with a verified, transparent ad supply chain.

To limit ad personalization:

  • iOS — ATT: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > toggle MetroTune off. This revokes IDFA access and switches ads to non-personalized.
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization (prevents the AdMob SDK from using the AAID).
  • EU/EEA/UK: The Google UMP consent form is shown at first launch. To change your consent choice, contact us at privacy@archetstudio.com to request a consent reset.

8. Privacy Manifest & Required Reason APIs

MetroTune™ is developed in accordance with Apple's Privacy Manifest policy. The application implements the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and requests IDFA access for ad personalization purposes.

  • NSPrivacyTracking: Enabled. MetroTune™ implements the ATT framework and presents an ATT permission prompt at first launch. The IDFA is accessed only if the user grants permission.
  • NSPrivacyTrackingDomains: Applicable. Google/AdMob tracking domains are declared in the Privacy Manifest in accordance with Apple's requirements.
  • Required Reason APIs: MetroTune™ uses UserDefaults (via AsyncStorage) strictly for storing user preferences such as color theme and premium unlock timestamps. File Timestamp APIs, if accessed, are used solely for cache management of bundled assets. These APIs are never used for device fingerprinting or cross-app identification.

All current and future releases adhere to these Privacy Manifest and Data Minimization principles.

9. Device Permissions

MetroTune™ may request the following device permissions:

iOS

  • Microphone: Required for real-time audio analysis features such as pitch detection (tuner) and optional hands-free control via double-clap detection (metronome).
  • Microphone (Background): Used for real-time audio analysis while the app is in the foreground. Background audio is not enabled; playback and microphone listening stop when the app is backgrounded or the screen is off.

Android

  • Record Audio: Required for real-time audio analysis features such as pitch detection (tuner) and optional double-clap detection (metronome).
  • Modify Audio Settings: Required for audio routing during simultaneous playback and recording.
  • Foreground Service: Keeps the microphone-based audio analysis active while the app is in the foreground. On Android 14+, the sub-type FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE is declared for real-time pitch detection and double-clap listening. The app does not use a media-playback foreground service; metronome audio stops when the app is backgrounded.
  • Internet: Required by the AdMob SDK for ad delivery.

10. Your Rights

The only data Archet Studio LLC directly holds is information you voluntarily provide via email correspondence to privacy@archetstudio.com.

All locally stored preferences are automatically deleted when the user uninstalls the application. For data collected by the AdMob SDK, users may refer to Google's privacy tools to manage their data.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You may request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To submit a data deletion request, email privacy@archetstudio.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  • Right to Correct: Under CPRA § 1798.106, you may request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not "sell" personal information. However, our use of the Google AdMob SDK on Android may be considered "sharing" for cross-contextual behavioral advertising purposes under the CPRA. You may opt out by adjusting your device settings or ATT permissions (see Section 7).
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined under the CPRA (e.g., Social Security numbers, financial account details, precise geolocation, racial or ethnic origin, biometric data for identification).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your California privacy rights.

Authorized Agent

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. To do so, provide a signed, written authorization to the agent and contact us at privacy@archetstudio.com so we can verify your identity and the agent's authority.

Financial Incentive Disclosure

MetroTune™ offers a rewarded ad value exchange in which users may watch a personalized video advertisement to receive a 24-hour premium content unlock (see Section 4.4). This exchange requires the processing of advertising identifiers by the AdMob SDK. Participation is entirely voluntary, and you may use the application without participating. The value of the premium content unlock is reasonably related to the value of the data involved in serving the personalized advertisement.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@archetstudio.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request; we will ask you to confirm the email address from which your inquiry was sent. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

European Economic Area (EEA) / GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with additional rights regarding your personal data.

Data Controller

Archet Studio LLC is the data controller for personal data it directly collects, specifically email correspondence and website contact interactions. For data collected through the Google AdMob SDK, Google LLC acts as an independent data controller and processes that data in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Relied upon for EEA/UK users. MetroTune™ implements Google UMP, which presents a consent form to EEA/UK users at first launch. Personalized advertising is served to EEA/UK users only with explicit consent. On iOS, ATT permission is additionally required. You may withdraw consent at any time (see "Right to Withdraw Consent" below).
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): Non-personalized advertisement delivery (where consent is not given or not applicable) and email correspondence to respond to inquiries.

Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability — receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing of your personal data based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw Consent — withdraw previously granted consent at any time (see below)
  • Lodge a Complaint — file a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@archetstudio.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by the GDPR.

International Data Transfers

Data collected by the AdMob SDK may be transferred to Google servers located in the United States and other countries outside the EEA. These transfers are governed by the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and/or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as implemented by Google. For more information, see Google's data transfer frameworks.

EU Representative

Archet Studio LLC is a small business whose processing of EEA personal data is occasional, does not include large-scale processing, and does not involve special categories of data or data relating to criminal convictions. Accordingly, we rely on the exemption under GDPR Article 27(2) and have not appointed an EU representative. You may contact us directly at privacy@archetstudio.com for any data protection inquiries.

Automated Decision-Making

Archet Studio LLC does not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects. The AdMob SDK may use automated processes to select and serve personalized advertisements; for details, refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

Right to Withdraw Consent / Limit Ad Personalization

You may withdraw or limit ad personalization consent at any time:

  • iOS — ATT: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > toggle MetroTune off. Ads will immediately switch to non-personalized.
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • EU/EEA/UK — UMP consent: To reset your consent choice, contact us at privacy@archetstudio.com to request a consent reset. We will respond within 30 days.
